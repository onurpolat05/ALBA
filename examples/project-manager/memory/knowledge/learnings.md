## Entries

### Client Communication Timing
**Context:** Acme Corp escalation in Sprint 2 - client was surprised by a delay
**Insight:** Sending status updates proactively (even when news is bad) builds trust. Silence creates anxiety.
**Application:** Always send weekly updates on Wednesday, even if status is unchanged. Flag risks as soon as identified, not at the next scheduled update.

### Sprint Velocity Calibration
**Context:** Sprint 1-3 velocity tracking showed consistent over-commitment
**Insight:** Team averages 42 story points per sprint, not the planned 50. Planning at 85% capacity accounts for interruptions and unplanned work.
**Application:** Plan sprints at 42-44 points. Reserve 15% buffer for urgent items and tech debt.

### 1:1 Structure
**Context:** Initial 1:1s were too unstructured, missed important topics
**Insight:** A lightweight agenda (blockers, wins, growth, feedback) keeps 1:1s productive without making them rigid.
**Application:** Use the 4-point template for all 1:1s. Let the team member drive the conversation but ensure blockers are always covered first.
