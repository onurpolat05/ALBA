# Business Learnings

## Client Payment Patterns - 2026-03-04
**Context:** Acme delayed payment twice
**Insight:** Always collect 50% upfront for projects > $3K. Net-15 terms, not net-30.
**Application:** Update contract template, enforce before starting new projects

## MVP Scope Control - 2026-03-02
**Context:** StartupX scope creep in week 2
**Insight:** Define "what's NOT in the MVP" explicitly in kickoff. Clients assume features unless explicitly excluded.
**Application:** Add "Out of Scope" section to every project brief
